Who We Are

Empowering Communities
in Suba North

Tumaini Dice CBO is a community-based organisation rooted in Mbita Town, Suba North Sub-County, Homa Bay County. We work alongside community members to improve livelihoods, protect the environment and champion equal access to essential services.

🌅 Our Vision

To build a resilient, healthy and empowered community where all people have equal access to essential services, opportunities and sustainable livelihoods.

🎯 Our Mission

To improve the quality of living of community members through integrated development initiatives focusing on water access, education support, health awareness, environmental conservation and economic empowerment.

What We Stand For

Our Objectives

Eight pillars that guide every project, partnership and programme we undertake.

01

Economic empowerment and resource mobilization for future projects.

02

Sustainable access to clean and safe water through spring protection, borehole construction and improved sanitation systems.

03

Environmental conservation and climate change mitigation through tree planting, waste management and sustainable farming practices.

04

Support members during calamities; promote health awareness including HIV/AIDS prevention, mental health and SGBV advocacy.

05

Promote empathy, teamwork and responsibility; ensure education access for girls, young mothers and persons with disability.

06

Create opportunities for members to improve their socio-economic conditions through viable income-generating activities.

07

Youth and women empowerment through skills development and income generating activities.

08

Promote fairness and accountability in all business and organisational affairs.

What We Do

Our Activities

On-the-ground programmes that directly transform lives in our community.

💧

Water Projects

Springs, boreholes and water point construction to ensure clean, safe water access for all community members.

📚

Training & Capacity Building

Workshops and training sessions that equip community members with practical skills for sustainable livelihoods.

🌳

Environmental Conservation

Tree planting campaigns, waste management drives and sustainable farming advocacy to protect Lake Victoria's ecosystem.

🏥

Health Awareness Campaigns

Community outreach on HIV/AIDS, mental health, SGBV and general wellness to build a healthier population.

🎓

Education Support

Scholarships, mentorship and learning support targeting girls, young mothers and persons with disability.

🤝

Partnerships & Donors

Collaboration with stakeholders, NGOs and donors to amplify impact and mobilise resources for community projects.

💼

Income Generating Activities

Supporting women and youth in entrepreneurship, group savings and small-business development for financial independence.
